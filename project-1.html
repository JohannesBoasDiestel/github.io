About the Project

VOD is a story based Souls-like RPG game, designed with immersive NPC interactions, and an unforgiving combat system. Immerse yourself in the world of characters and stories, and experience the unraveling of the world within.

Set in a time where humankind has become subservient to the Dragons which rule from the void, prophecy foretells a reckoning draws nigh, a vessel is born, a bridge from the Abyss into the world of humankind.
